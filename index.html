A little bit about me

When I finished college with my public health degree, I spent a year working in that industry when I began to feel that my career was missing something. I felt as if I was stagnant and wasn't encountering any growth. I yearned for more challenging situations that would require me to learn and grow. This led me to finding out about code!

After learning about web development, I spent much of my time reading and studying the career and life in this industry. After months of this research and deciding this is exactly what I needed, I began my journey at Thinkful!

I am beyond excited to learn and grow as a web developer and can't wait to work with a team that pushes each other to grow. I am naturally a people person so I am eager to work with different people to collaborate and create new ideas. Like the old saying goes, two heads are better than one!

When I'm not studying or working on code, I love being outside. I am an avid hiker, fisher, and if I had it my way, I would be camping every weekend. Nature and code work together as the loop that brings me joy in my life.
